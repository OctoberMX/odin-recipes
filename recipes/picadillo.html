Picadillo

Ingredients:

  • 500 g of ground beef
  • 100 g of potatoes
  • 100 g of carrots
  • 100 g of zucchini
  • 1 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1 cup of water
  • Olives (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the beef in an oiled pan. Put a lid on so it cooks faster and retains the meat juice.
  2. Chop the potatos, carrot and zucchini in small pieces.
  3. Once the beef is dark, pour the tomato sauce and water.
  4. Once the sauce is boiling, add the vegetables in this order: potatoes, carrots and zucchini, two minutes apart from each other.
  5. Turn off the flame after 10 minutes and let it rest for another 5. Add olives to taste!
  6. TIP: Best served with white rice!
