Chicken and Potatoes Mole

Ingredients:

  • 500 g of chicken breast
  • 300 g of potatoes
  • 100 g of mole paste

Instructions:

  1. Heat water until it boils, then place the pieces of chicken breast in.
  2. While the chicken cooks, dice the potatoes in medium-size pieces.
  3. Once the chicken is done, take it out of the boiling water and let ir rest for a moment. Don't throw the chicken broth away! We'll use it later on.
  4. Once it cooled down, shred the chicken with your hands.
  5. Put the mole paste on a slightly oiled pan and once it starts cooking pour some chicken broth little by little until the paste completely dissolves in a thick sauce.
  6. Add the potatoes and let them cook for 15 minutes.
  7. Turn off the fire and serve with white rice.
